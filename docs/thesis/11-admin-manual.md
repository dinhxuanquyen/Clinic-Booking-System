# 11 - Admin Manual

## Dashboard

Admin Dashboard dung de theo doi:

- Tong lich hen.
- Benh nhan.
- Bac si.
- Co so.
- Waiting list.
- Medical records.
- Yeu cau can xu ly.
- Bieu do phan tich va audit gan day.

## Quan Ly Co So

1. Vao Co so.
2. Them/sua/xoa co so.
3. Cap nhat dia chi, dien thoai, email, hinh anh.

## Quan Ly Chuyen Khoa

1. Vao Chuyen khoa.
2. Them/sua/xoa chuyen khoa.
3. Cap nhat mo ta va hinh anh.

## Quan Ly Bac Si

1. Vao Bac si.
2. Tao tai khoan/ho so bac si.
3. Gan co so va chuyen khoa.
4. Cap nhat hoc vi, kinh nghiem, avatar, trang thai.

## Quan Ly Lich Hen

1. Vao Lich hen.
2. Loc theo trang thai, co so, bac si.
3. Xac nhan lich.
4. Xu ly yeu cau huy/doi lich.

## Quan Ly Goi Kham Va Bai Viet

1. Vao Goi kham hoac Cam nang.
2. Tao noi dung moi.
3. Kiem tra trang thai hien thi.
4. Cap nhat thong tin theo nhu cau van hanh.

## Audit Log

Audit log dung de theo doi hanh dong quan tri va cac su kien quan trong trong he thong.

Khi bao ve do an, nen mo audit log de chung minh he thong co kha nang giam sat.
