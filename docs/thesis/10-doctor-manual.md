# 10 - Doctor Manual

## Dang Nhap

2. Neu la lan dau, he thong yeu cau doi mat khau.
3. Sau khi doi mat khau, vao Doctor Portal.

## Hang Doi Kham

1. Vao Hang doi kham.
2. Xem danh sach benh nhan theo ngay.
3. Xac nhan lich.
4. Bat dau kham.
5. Hoan thanh hoac danh dau khong den neu can.

## Tao Ho So Kham

1. Mo lich dang kham.
2. Nhap thong tin sinh ton neu co.
3. Nhap trieu chung, chan doan, ket luan.
4. Nhap don thuoc neu co.
5. Nhap ke hoach tai kham neu can.
6. Luu ho so kham.

## Quan Ly Lich Lam Viec

1. Vao Lich lam viec cua toi.
2. Cau hinh lich mac dinh theo tuan.
3. Tao ngay nghi hoac thay doi lich neu can.
4. Dung Calendar view de kiem tra tong quan.
